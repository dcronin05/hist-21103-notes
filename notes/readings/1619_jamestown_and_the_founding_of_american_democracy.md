# 1619: Jamestown and the Founding of American Democracy

*By James Horn (2018)*

This is the required reading text for UALR HIST 21103. The book explores the early history of the Virginia colony, focusing on the year 1619 as the intersection of representative democracy (the House of Burgesses) and the introduction of unfree labor (the first enslaved Africans).
